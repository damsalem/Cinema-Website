About Academy Cinemas

Located in the beautiful city of Portland, Oregon's Pearl District!

Our reels have been spinning since 1959. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is what drives us.

Accommodations

All of our accommodations are equipped with a brand new Christie 3D © sound system!

Relax in our extra-wide, ultra-plush cushioned recliners. Seating is a first-come, first-served basis. Our seating arrangement is disability friendly ♿

Our auditoriums are climate controlled to approximately 72 year around.
